Installation

Arduino
Laser Cut
MAX4466 Microphone Amplifier
Addressable WS2812b LEDs
Acrylic Sheets
2019

The purpose of this installation is to detect and visualize the sound in city in order to express the vitality in city. Heart is a visualization of life. When the sensor detect the sound around, it will trigger the blink of the lights to simulate the heartbeat.
Arduino is attached to a sound sensor and a soldered LED strip, it will receive the sound level and visualize it according to the number of LEDs. A switch is installed on the side of the lower case to control the installation. Laser cut acrylic sheets are installed vertically to the light strip, and the light will be refracted and reflected throught the acrylic.

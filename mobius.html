Discovering the conflicts of identity seeking for cyborgs. The main concept is "Artificial intelligence is the invention of humankind that reflect the human desire of endless identity seeking". Research is based on two films, Blader Runner and Ghost In the Shell. According to the research, it comes to the conculsion that identity seeking for cyborgs is infinite and endless with struggling and painful conflicts.
The title "Möbius" comes from möbius strip which is a surface with only one side and only one boundary. It has property of unorientable and infinite that fit in the concept about identity seeking.

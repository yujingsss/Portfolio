Literature Adaptation
Adobe After Effects
2020

Adapted from Virginia Woolf's novel The Waves, 1931. Focus on the emotional changes and the mooods depicted in the literature, and extracted representative objects decsribed in the novel. The setup including two projectors, facing each other. One projector is projectinf abstract colors and visuals represent the mood, another projector is projecting specific visuals according to the emotions and objects raised in the novel. Setting up two projectors opposing each other with a silk fabric as the screen give the projection a soft and wavy look and also makes the environment look like the curtains in a house. The water jar in front of the projector creates the reflections resembling the water texture.
